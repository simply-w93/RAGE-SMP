Server Abilities

Killing a player drops an Ability Book, which grants a random ability when used. There are 12 abilities across 4 categories.

Movement Abilities

Shadow Step

Cooldown: 50s

Teleports 8-10 blocks forward and blinds enemies within a 6-block radius for 6 seconds.

Lightning Dash

Cooldown: 50s

Dashes 12 blocks forward, dealing 2 hearts of damage to any enemy hit.

Defense Abilities

Power Shield

Cooldown: 90s

Grants 20 absorption hearts and Resistance II for 10 seconds.

Iron Guard

Cooldown: 60s

Knocks enemies back 10 blocks, dealing 2 hearts of damage and granting you Regeneration and Resistance II for 8 seconds.
