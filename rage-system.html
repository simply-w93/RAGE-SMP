The Rage System

Rage is the core of this server. It dictates your strength, your speed, and even how many abilities you can use. You start at Rage Level 5. Killing a player steals 1 Rage, while dying makes you lose 1. If you die 5 times at Rage Level 1, you will be death-banned until revived with a Revive Beacon.

Ability Slots

Your Rage Level also determines how many abilities you can equip at once:

  • Rage 1-3: 1 Ability Slot
  • Rage 4-7: 2 Ability Slots
  • Rage 8-10: 3 Ability Slots
